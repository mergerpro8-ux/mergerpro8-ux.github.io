Introduction

Pdf-LegalDan Pro ("the App"), also available as PDF Merger Pro on Google Play, is developed and operated by LegalDan Software ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All PDF processing happens entirely on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our App is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Document contents or PDF files
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files (beyond files you explicitly select for PDF processing)

Local Processing Only

All PDF operations — including merging, splitting, editing, signing, and bundling — are performed entirely on your device. Your documents are never uploaded to external servers or cloud services.

The App does not require an internet connection to perform its core PDF functions.

Device Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Purchase status: Encrypted record of your subscription or pass status, stored securely using AES encryption.
  • App preferences: Language setting (Hebrew/English) and display preferences.
  • Saved signatures: Digital signatures you create are stored locally and never transmitted.

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional subscriptions and passes through Apple's App Store and Google Play Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details.

For information about how Apple or Google handles your payment data, please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-CBC encryption with HMAC integrity verification for stored data
  • Device binding to prevent unauthorized access
  • Root/jailbreak detection for enhanced security
  • No external network communication for PDF processing

File Access Permissions

The App may request permission to access your device's photo library. This permission is used solely to allow you to select images for PDF conversion. We do not access files beyond those you explicitly choose.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, the App is safe for all ages (rated 4+).

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party interaction is with Apple's App Store or Google Play for in-app purchase processing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
